Honey Sriracha Ground Chicken and Broccoli Bowl

This ground chicken and broccoli recipe is the perfect blend of both sweet and spicy, all bundled into one simple to make package. Simple enough to be made in under 30 minutes with the availability of preparing with either rice or noodles. Give this recipe a try and you will come back wanting more!

Needed Ingredients

  • 2 Tablespoon Olive Oil
  • 12 oz Broccoli Florets - Cut into bite sized pieces
  • 1 pound Ground Chicken
  • 1/4 Cup Sweet Chili Sauce
  • 2 Tablespoons Honey
  • 2-3 Tablespoons Sriracha
  • 2 Tablespoons Toasted Sesame Oil
  • 1 Teaspoon Garlic Powder
  • 1 Teaspoon Ground Ginger
  • Sesame Seeds and Green Onions - For garnish

How To Make Honey Sriracha Ground Turkey with Broccoli

  1. Heat 1/2 Tablespoon of olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add in your broccoli and leave untouched for 1-2 minutes to begin browning on one side. Stir and continue cooking until the broccoli is a dark green, around 3-4 minutes longer. Move broccoli to a place and set aside.
  2. Add your remaining olive oil to the skillet and brown both sides of the ground chicken before mincing and fully cooking.
  3. While the chicken is cooking, mix the remaining sauce ingredients together. Add the sauce to the fully cooked chicken and cook until thick. Make sure to not burn during this step.
  4. Once the sauce is thick, add the broccoli back to the skillet and stir everything together. Garnish with sesame seeds and green onions before serving.

One of my personal favorite things about this recipe is that it's all up to you! You can choose the perfect amount of sweet, and the perfect amount of spice. This recipe seems to be a weekly staple for me and my family, make it yourself and you may soon find out why!
