Vegetable Beef Soup

Vegetable Beef Soup is an easy to make tomato based soup recipe with ground beef (or ground turkey for healthier option), seasonings, potatoes and frozen/fresh vegetables. This soup uses only a small amount of ingredients and can be make in one pot with ease. For a simpler meal opt for frozen vegetables or to make this recipe your own use fresh vegetables. Try for an alternative of ground turkey for a healthier option for the family.

Who doesn't love a nice hearty soup recipe, especially on a nice cold winter day. This meal has stood the test of time. Although it may fall under all sorts of different names around the world, the idea of this recipe remains the same. This has been a stable in my family for many years, try it out and watch it become a staple for your's as well!

Ingredients You Will Need

  • Ground Beef (ground turkey as alternative) - I personally use ground turkey for this recipe and my family loves it, if opting for ground beef then I would recommend 93/7 lean ground beef.
  • Onion - Use a yellow or white onion for this recipe.
  • Garlic - I recommend using fresh garlic cloves for this recipe, but one can also use chopped garlic from a jar, or garlic paste from a tube in this recipe.
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Chopped Gold Potatoes - Peel the potatoes and chop into really small cube sized pieces.
  • Beef Broth - For the heart healthy folks such as myself, the low sodium is the best option to use in this recipe. For that extra flavor opt for the full-salt beef broth.
  • Condensed Tomato Soup
  • Petite Diced Tomatoes
  • Green Beans - Fresh is the way to go in my option, for simplicity sake opt for the frozen green
  • Mixed Vegetables - I personally opt for the fresh vegetables in this recipe and use green beans, corn, peas, celery, carrots and squash. If you want this to be a quick dinner options, you can opt for a bag of the frozen vegetables instead. Look for an array of at least carrots, peas, corn, and green beans.
  • Fresh or Dried Parsley
  • Fresh or Dried Basil
